# Nano Banana 2 — Style Transfer Prompt (Identity-Locked)

**How to use:** Upload two images in Google AI Studio.
- **Image 1 = Reference** (the style, outfit, background, pose you want to copy)
- **Image 2 = Subject** (the person whose face & identity must stay the same)

Then paste the prompt below.

---

## ✦ The Prompt

> Use **Image 1** as the *style and aesthetic reference only*, and **Image 2** as the *person whose identity must be fully preserved*.
>
> Take everything from Image 1 — the color gradient background, the overall color tone and grading, the lighting mood, the radiant/shimmering fabric outfit and its texture and drape, the aesthetic, and the general visual appearance — and apply all of it to the person in Image 2.
>
> Match the pose, stance, body posture, head tilt, hand placement, and facial expression (including the smile and the direction of the gaze) from Image 1, so the person in Image 2 is positioned and styled exactly like the subject in Image 1.
>
> **Critical identity lock — do NOT change these from Image 2:**
> - The face must remain 100% identical to Image 2 — same facial structure, same features, same proportions. The face is how this person is recognized, so it must not be altered, beautified, reshaped, or replaced.
> - Keep the same skin tone, hair (style, length, color), beard, eyebrows, and overall head/face shape exactly as they appear in Image 2.
> - Keep the same body type and build as in Image 2.
>
> **Framing rule:** Match the exact crop and framing of Image 1. If Image 1 shows the subject from the chest to below the navel, or down to the knees, etc., reproduce that same shot extent in the final image. The zoom level, camera angle, and distance from subject must match Image 1.
>
> **Final result:** A photorealistic, high-quality, professionally lit and color-graded portrait where the *person from Image 2* appears in the *style, outfit, pose, background, and mood of Image 1* — clearly still recognizable as the same individual from Image 2, with no changes to their face, hair, beard, or body.
>
> Render in sharp, high resolution with natural, realistic detail.

---

## ✦ Follow-up fixes (if the first result is off)

- **Face changed too much:**
  *"The face changed too much — regenerate keeping the face pixel-for-pixel identical to Image 2, only changing the outfit, background, and pose."*

- **Wrong outfit color:**
  Name the exact color, e.g. *"emerald-green shimmering satin"* or *"rose-gold radiant fabric."*

- **Wrong crop/zoom:**
  *"Match the framing of Image 1 exactly — same crop from [chest / waist / knees], same camera distance."*

- **Background not matching:**
  *"Replace the background with the exact gradient and color tone from Image 1."*

---

## ✦ Tips

- Nano Banana 2 responds best to **concrete visual instructions**, not role-play setups ("expert team" framing isn't needed).
- Be specific about colors, fabric type, and crop level — the more precise, the closer the match.
- Generate a few times; pick the best and refine with a short follow-up rather than rewriting the whole prompt.
